Spaghetti Pasta Sauce

Description

A fresh and modern pasta sauce, which is certain to mix up everyone's favourite dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup prepared mincemeat, crumbled, or to taste
  • 1 (28 ounce) jar prepared pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree, or to taste (optional)
  • 5 leaves fresh basil, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil over meadium heat in a pot
  2. Cook and stir onion until slightly browned, around 10 minutes
  3. Add and stir the mincemeat, until well mixed with onion
  4. Pour in pasta sauce and tomato puree, and mix withh basil leaves
  5. Bring sauce to a simmer. Cook until flavours have blended
  6. Stir for around 10 minutes, and serve.
